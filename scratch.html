I found some cool data on Walmart

  1. Interesting facts about Walmart
    • Walmart is the combination of two words. Walton (the founder's surname) and market.
    • There are over 600 Walmart stores in Texas. Following it is Florida and California, which have more than 300 stores each.
    • Up until 1991, all Walmart stores were located in the US. The first international Walmart was opened in Mexico City.

  2. Walmarts around the world
    • United States: 5,362 locations
    • Mexico: 2,448 locations
    • United Kingdom: 633 locations
    • China: 433 locations
    • Canada: 411 locations
    • South Africa: 390 locations
